About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Islamic University, Kushtia, is dedicated to providing a world-class education in the field of computer science. We strive to foster a learning environment that encourages innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that students receive the best possible preparation for their future careers.
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Islamic University, Kushtia is one of the leading departments in Bangladesh. The campus is located at Shantidanga-Dulalpur, beside the Kushtia-Khulna highway and about 24 kms south and 22 kms north of respectively from the Kushtia and Jhenidah district-towns. The Department is primarily committed to serving students who have set a goal of obtaining a degree in computer science and engineering.
